Bible Memorization Tool

This tutorial will guide you through using different aspects of the Bible Verse Memorization App.

This app is created to help you improve your Bible memorization skills.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Getting Started

When you first open the app, you should see this navigation bar:

To get started, looking on the right side of the navigation bar, you should see a toggle switch, a difficulty selector, and a version selector.

The green toggle switch controls sound effects, simply click on the switch to turn it off and on.

The selection tool labeled "Easy" lets you choose the level of difficulty. This will be explained further in Step 3.

To select which translation you would like to use, you can look at the dropdown next to the difficulty selector and select the preferred language/translation you would like to use.

Step 2: Selecting a verse

On the left hand side of the navigation bar, you should see a small box and a green book icon.

This section lets you select the verse you want to see. You can either type in the verse reference (e.g. Genesis 1:1), or you can use the little book icon next to it to select the verse.

If you choose to type in the verse reference, you can also type in shorthand versions of the book (e.g. Gen or Gn for Genesis)

Another way to select your desired Verse is by clicking on the green book button next to the text box. After clicking on it, you should see a screen like this:

The verse search option gives three categories to browse through.

Top verses will display different verses based on themes. For example in the example above the themes listed include Love, Lord, God, Prayer, etc...

The Bible tab will give you a selection screen to let you choose the Bible verse you would like to see displayed.

The Pericopes option will show you different pericopes available throughout the Bible.

Click on your desired verse or pericope to quiz yourself on. The text should appear in the white area under the navigation bar.

Step 3: Beginning the Quiz

Now that you have selected a verse/pericope, you can begin playing. In the navigation bar clock on the orange play button. This will shuffle the text around.

The verse you chose will break down into different segments, and then shuffle itself up. The Difficulty you selected in Step 1 will determine how fragmentary the segments will be. The harder the level, the more segments the verse will be split up and shuffled.

If you chose a pericope, the sentences within the pericope will be shuffled. The Difficulty level is disabled when pericope is selected, since it shuffles by sentences, not by words.

To play, simply put the shuffled verses/sentences in order. If the text is in the right place, the yellow box will disappear and the white background will return.

To return to the original verse, the stop button (where the play button was) will return it to the original text.
